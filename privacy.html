Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
✅ No personal data collected

MP GK Portal ("App", "we", "our") is a free educational application designed for MPPSC (Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission) exam preparation. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Google Account Info: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This is used only to identify your account and personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: Your quiz scores, study streak, flagged questions, and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not send this data to any server.
  • Payment Info: If you purchase premium access, payments are processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store your card or payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via Google Sign-In
  • To manage your 7-day free trial and premium subscription
  • To check for app updates from our GitHub repository
  • To display your name and photo in the app

3. Data Storage

All your study progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (Firebase Auth): For user authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Razorpay: For processing premium payments. Subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub: For hosting app update files. No personal data is shared.

5. Advertising

Our app does not display any advertisements and does not use any advertising networks or tracking SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Authentication is handled entirely by Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your account info by tapping your profile in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in Android settings.
  • Sign Out: You can sign out anytime from the account menu in the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
